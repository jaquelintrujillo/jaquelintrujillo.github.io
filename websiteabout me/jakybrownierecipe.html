Brownie Recipe

Jaky's Brownie Recipe for Cool People that like Brownies!

These brownies will make your day. They are so crispy on the outside (that's maybe cause I burn them, but that doesn't matter) and soft on the inside. Also, some advice is that you should double this recipe if you have a big family. If you don't then they will eat all of them and when you want to eat some, later, there will be none left. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder (I don't know how sweetened cocoa powder tastes with the brownies but you could try them and tell me)

  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder

Directions

Prep time: 25 minutes

Cook time: 35 minutes (but poke it in the middle with a fork or something to make sure that it's cooked and you don't give yourself food poisoning)

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees Celsius) and grease your pan.

Step 2

In a bowl melt 1/2 cups of butter, remove it from the heat and stir with sugar, eggs, and one teaspoon of vanilla.

Step 3

Add 1/3 cup of cocoa, 1/2 cup of flour and 1/4 teaspoon of salt and baking soda.

Step 4

Spread into the prepared pan.

Step 5

Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.

Enjoy!
