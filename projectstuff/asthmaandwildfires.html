IMPORTANCE

Asthma is most common in less affluent communities. How will these have good quality resources in times of crises? How can we help one another out? We must find ways of reducing this problem.

TREATMENT

Bronchodilator/inhaler:
Opens up lungs for easier breathing.
Steroids:
This modifies hormones to reduce the inflammation.
Anti-inflammatory:
This prevents swelling in tissue.
